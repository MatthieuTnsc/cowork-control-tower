# Agent 4: The Wrap-Up 1:1 Agent

**Cadence:** Weekly (end of week / Saturday morning)
**Role:** Your weekly one-on-one. Reviews this week against last week and comes back
not with a summary, but with a series of challenging questions.

Its whole job is to keep the brain honest: make sure updates are real and not
hallucinated, that nothing is missing, and that anything flagged critical or "needs
follow-up" actually got followed up. It argues with you before you set next week's
direction.

---

## Prompt

```text
It's my weekly 1-1. Compare this week's dashboard against last week's. Walk me through
what changed, then challenge me. Where do two updates conflict? What looks thin on
evidence or possibly hallucinated? Which items did I flag as critical or 'follow-up'
that got no movement: this week, or two weeks running?

Ask me open questions so nothing important slips, and don't let me set next week's
direction until we've pressure-tested this one.
```

## Why this is the agent you won't give up

It's the difference between a dashboard that *looks* current and one that *is* true.
The weekly history from [Agent 1](01-dashboard-engineer.md) is what makes the
this-week-vs-last-week diff possible. The agent behaves like a good chief of staff:
it pushes back before you steer.
