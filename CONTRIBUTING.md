# Contributing

This started as one person's tailor-made setup. The whole reason it's open is to make
it better with other people's contexts. All input is welcome.

## Ways to contribute

- **Share how you adapted it.** Open a Discussion describing your role, your toolset,
  and what you changed. These stories are the most valuable thing here.
- **Improve a prompt.** If you found wording that makes an agent more reliable, open a
  PR against the relevant file in [`/prompts`](prompts).
- **Extend the dashboard.** New tabs, better data model, cleaner rendering. Keep the
  "single self-contained file, one data object" principle.
- **Report a gap.** Where does the pattern break down? Open an Issue.

## Ground rules

- Keep all sample data fictional. Never commit real names, figures, ticket IDs or
  anything organisation-specific.
- Keep it simple. The strength of this pattern is that it's one HTML file and four
  prompts, not a platform.
- Be kind and constructive.

## Not sure where to start?

Open a Discussion and say hello. Tell us what part of your week you'd most want to
hand to an agent.
